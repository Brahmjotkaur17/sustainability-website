Carbon Emission Report


This report will provide a detailed analysis on the carbon footprint generation through air travel by the international students this year. In order to achieve the goal of net zero carbon emissions by 2050, we will have to work hard to promote sustainable practices at both personal and institutional levels.

The analysis has been done of basis of a few assumptions :
  • International Students travel fly just once from their home country to dublin annually. But in reality, the frequency of travel is usually more.
  • All Students travel in the economy class. In reality, this could be true for majority students but not all.
